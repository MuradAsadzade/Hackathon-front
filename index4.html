An all-uphill short rewarding day out in Aug 2024 with 12yo and 8yo tagging along. 99% of the hike is trail through the forest, plenty of shade, and straight forward to follow if you're using the All Trails map. You can park at the trail head in a little picnic area just before the entrance to the Qalaalti Resort. There is a permanent map at the picnic area that shows the route too. It is worth noting that the trail ends just outside the walls on the "backside" (cliff side) of Chiraq Qala castle, so the last 50 meters or so was a pretty steep hands and feet rock climb/scramble to actually reach an entry door to the castle. The part of the castle we reached at the end of this hike is physically disconnected from the "main" castle by an air gap, and when we arrived there was ongoing construction to build (or rebuild?) a foot bridge between them. But because it was under active construction/repairs we were unable to cross over to the main castle, which was a little disappointing. Hopefully this construction will finish soon and provide a nice safe walkway to access the whole castle. But the views from even just the part we did reach were truly outstanding!
